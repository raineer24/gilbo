DIVING AND DIVE TRAINING

Gilboa Quarry is the best inland place to scuba dive in Northwest Ohio.

OPEN WATER DIVING

Gilboa Quarry is a great location for diving and dive training. The quarry provides two distinct personalities: the shallow side at 5 - 65 ft provides great recreational enjoyment, while the deep side of the quarry with depths of 130 plus ft is suitable for technical training.
